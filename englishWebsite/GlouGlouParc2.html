GLOUGLOU-PARC

A mobile game created during a Game Jam organized by the company Tap-Nation

A mobile game

GlouGlou-Parc is a mobile clicker-type game in the theme of amusement parks. This game also takes on a contemplative aspect thanks to the designs created by the artists present on the project and the music which creates a calm atmosphere allowing you to enjoy the scenery. You must therefore click on the main stand of your amusement park to earn money and improve or create new stands to expand your amusement park.

A Game Jam Project

This project was created for a Game Jam organized at the Gaming Campus school in Lyon by the company TapNation specialized in mobile games, and therefore we had to create a mobile game in the theme of amusement parks.
