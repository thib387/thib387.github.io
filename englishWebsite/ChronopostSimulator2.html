CHRONOPOST-SIMULATOR

A parcel delivery simulation game created for a Game Jam

The Project

For the Beginner's Jam Winter 2024, whose theme was “Delivery”, we decided to create a parcel delivery game to match the theme, but it wasn’t fun enough. So we came up with Chronopost Simulator, a game reproducing how your packages are delivered by the Chronopost delivery company.

  • Game Jam Page

Express... and chaotic deliveries!

In Chronopost Simulator, you play as an overwhelmed delivery driver who must deliver all packages before the end of the day. Between heavy traffic and unexpected obstacles, every mission becomes a race against the clock.
The goal: deliver as fast as possible… without breaking everything!
